About Me

Creative Vision
Meets Craft

I am a fashion and portrait photographer based in New York, specializing in editorial, commercial, and fine art photography. My work explores the intersection of identity, light, and emotion — crafting images that linger long after the shutter clicks.

With clients ranging from luxury fashion houses to independent artists, I bring a meticulous eye and a collaborative spirit to every project. Every frame is a decision. Every shadow, intentional.
